About me

Rahul Wadhera is a highly successful Entrepreneur based in Dubai, United Arab Emirates. He has business interests in Construction, Real Estate , Luxury Hotels , Shopping Malls etc . Rahul has also won the prestigious award of Visionary Entrepreneur in Hospitality in the year 2018 from the government of UAE.

Businesses & Projects

Delmon Palace

Hotel Delmon Palace specializing in the best attraction in Dubai. Guests of Delmon Palace Hotel can easily experience some of Dubai’s well-known attractions. The popular attractions like Dream Journey (0.9 mi) and Museum of Illusions (1.2 mi), are all located within walking distance of the hotel. The facilities and services provided by the hotel ensure a pleasant stay for guests.

Ramada Plaza Dubai

Ramada Plaza Hotel Dubai is well known for its location which is in the historical Deira district of Dubai. The hotel has the best panoramic or courtyard views. Dining in the hotel combines a welcoming Arabian spirit with international style, at The Junction restaurant. The hotel provides first-rate recreation facilities at its Health Club for the exclusive use of their guests.

Delmon Boutique

Delmon Boutique hotel specializes for its design. The hotel is designed for both business and leisure travel. With an ideal location and facilities to match, The hotel hits the spot in many ways.Guests can make the most of all that the lively city has to offer. With its convenient location, the property offers easy access to the city's must-see destinations.

Gold Mining Project

The mining project is ongoing over several Gold mines in the central Africa.

Mohali City walk shopping mall

Mohali City walk shopping mall has become the go-to place for shopping lovers in city as it houses various Indian and international brands across its floors. It has also taken an initiative and is now playing a significant role in City’s modernization and urbanization by giving some of the most outstanding commercial developments
